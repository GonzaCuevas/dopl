How to Shop

Using Doppel to browse and buy from Taobao, 1688, and Weidian.

Doppel.fit helps you discover and assess products from Chinese marketplaces (Taobao, 1688, Weidian) by showing you product information and QC (Quality Control) photos from shipping agents. We don’t sell anything—we help you decide what to buy and where.

1. Search for products

Use the search bar on the homepage to find items by keyword, or paste a product link from Taobao, 1688, or Weidian. You can filter by category, platform, and popularity.

2. Review listings

On each product page you’ll see:

  • QC photos from agents (warehouse photos before shipping)
  • Seller history and similar items
  • Reviews and community feedback
  • Weight and estimated shipping details

3. Buy with an agent

When you’re ready to buy, use a shopping agent. Links labeled “Buy” on Doppel take you to external agent platforms. The agent will:

  • Order the item from the Chinese marketplace for you
  • Store it in their warehouse
  • Take QC photos
  • Ship your haul internationally

For detailed agent guides and comparisons, check our Blog.
